WHY CHOOSE MAMA NGINA UNIVERSITY COLLEGE?


We are a Student-Centered University College that produces innovative, scientifically grounded, ethical and holistic graduates. We offer market-driven degree programmes designed to enable students to develop hands-on skills necessary for job creation and employment at a competitive rate. MNUC has modern state-of-the-art teaching, learning, research, and recreation facilities.


Our students have access to personal and academic support that promote their holistic wellness thus enhancing and enriching their student experience. The University College is also located in a serene and scenic environment providing a conducive and friendly ambience for learning and social life.
